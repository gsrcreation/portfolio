About Me

I’m a graphic designer who enjoys turning ideas into clear, visually strong designs. I focus on balance, readability, and purpose rather than decoration. Alongside design, I’m learning technology to better understand how design and logic work together.

The snake in my symbol represents Python, the language I’m learning step by step. It reflects my curiosity, adaptability, and the courage to grow through challenges.

The gem carries my nickname and represents my belief in long-term growth. Like a real gem, it reminds me that value is created with time, effort, and patience.

Together, they capture my journey of blending creativity with technology, growing quietly but with purpose.
